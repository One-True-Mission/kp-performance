Accessibility Statement

Last updated: June 22, 2026

KP Performance & Automotive is committed to making our website usable for as many people as possible, including visitors who rely on assistive technologies. We want every customer to be able to learn about our services and reach us easily.

Our Commitment

We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA as a practical standard. Accessibility is an ongoing effort, and we work to improve the experience as we update the site.

What We Have Done

  • Semantic HTML structure so screen readers can navigate the content
  • Descriptive alt text on meaningful images
  • Color choices intended to provide readable contrast
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation and a clear focus indicator on form fields
  • A responsive layout that adapts to phones, tablets, and desktops
  • Reduced-motion support for visitors who prefer less animation

Ongoing Effort

We continue to review and refine the site over time. Some older content or features may not yet fully meet every guideline, and we appreciate your patience as we improve.

Third-Party Tools

Some parts of our site rely on third-party services, such as our contact form provider and web fonts. We do not control the accessibility of those tools, but we choose providers that support accessible use where we can.

Let Us Know

If you run into any difficulty using our website, or you need information in a different format, please tell us. We will do our best to help and to fix the issue.
