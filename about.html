Our Story

Built on Doing Things the Right Way

KP Performance & Automotive started with a love of performance and grew into a full-service shop for the whole community. We work on just about everything that comes through the door, from diesel trucks and daily drivers to classics and custom builds.

We know what it feels like to be taken for a ride when a mechanic hands you a quote. That is exactly why we do things differently. We tell you the truth about your vehicle, charge a fair price, and back our work.

That is also why we offer a free second opinion. Bring in a written estimate from anywhere and we will take an honest look, no pressure.
