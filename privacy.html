Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

KP Performance & Automotive ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have under the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA). By using this website or our services, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

We only collect information you choose to provide. When you submit our service form, we collect your name, phone number, email address, vehicle information, the service you need, and any details you include. When you bring a vehicle in, we also keep service and repair records. Our website may collect basic, non-identifying technical data through standard hosting logs or analytics.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your request and schedule service
  • To prepare estimates and perform the work you authorize
  • To maintain repair and service history for your vehicle
  • To contact you about your vehicle or your appointment

How Your Information Is Shared

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not process it for targeted advertising or profiling. We share information only as needed to operate, including with:

  • Formspree, the third-party service that delivers our form submissions to us
  • Our website hosting provider, which stores the site and basic server logs
  • Parts suppliers or sublet vendors when needed to complete a repair
  • Authorities when required by law

Your Texas Privacy Rights

Under the TDPSA, Texas residents may request to confirm whether we process their personal data, access that data, correct it, delete it, or obtain a portable copy. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. We will respond as required by law. If we deny a request, you may appeal by contacting us again.

Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies needed for it to function and, where applicable, to understand general usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work as well.

Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as needed to serve you, maintain repair records, and meet legal or accounting requirements, then dispose of it securely.

Children's Privacy

Our services and website are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.
